DIGGING DEEPER

What can you learn from Jesus about how he faced suffering and injustice?

What did Peter say when he was asked if he was one of Jesus' disciples? Why do you think he answered this way?

Imagine how Peter felt in this story? When have you felt similar emotions? How did you respond?

Spend some time talking to God about your personal experiences regarding suffering, injustice and denial. Allow God to lead you to an appropriate response.
